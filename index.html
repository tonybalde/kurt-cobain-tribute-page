Kurt Cobain

The Grunge's Father

Time Line Kurt Cobain's Life:

  • 1967 - Born in Aberdeen, Washington
  • 1969 - Began singing
  • 1971 - Began playing the piano while singing
  • 1976 - His parents divorced, and his attitude changed dramatically
  • June 28, 1979 - His mother granted full custody to his father; rebellious teenager; pawned off to Jesse Reed's 'born-again Christian' family as his father couldn't handle him. Middle school through High school - His father enrolled him in wrestling and baseball despite Kurt's hate for sports; He would intentionally lose to piss his father off; He was bullied at school for being friends with a homosexual; He liked being known as a gay even though he wasn't gay since he didn't like people in general; He drawings mostly consisted of the human anatomy; Began living with his mother for his last 3 years of High School; Dropped out after knowing he didn't have enough credits; Kicked out of his mother's house for dropping out and not finding employment.
  • 1983 - His family and friends swear his first concert was a Sammy Hagar and Quarterflash concert held at Seattle Center Coliseum; Kurt Cobain swears his first concert was a Melvins concert; He found his escape through the Pacific Northwest punk scene.
  • 1986 - Moved into an apartment after being homeless; Worked at a Polynesian coastal resort; Dated Tracy Marander after going to Olympia, Washington for rock concerts; Wrote 'About a Girl' about Marander; Dated Tobi Vail from the bands 'Riot Grrrl' and 'Bikini Kill' after Marander.
  • 1990 - Worked on a musical project with Vail called 'Bathtub is Real' which was recorded on Vail's father's four-track tape; Hannah, from Bikini Kill, spray-painted 'Kurt smells like teen spirit' on his apartment wall after talking about anarchism and punk rock; Teen spirit is a deodorant brand Vail used, but Kurt thought the phrase as 'revolutionary'.
  • 1991 - Formed the band 'Nirvana' with Novoselic and Grohl; Sold their best album 'Nevermind'; Inadvertantly created a subgenere of Punk-rock called 'Grunge'.
  • 1992 - He married Courtney Love as they both were infatuated with each other, and both loved doing drugs with each other.
  • April 10, 1994 - Kurt Cobain was found dead at age 27 by an electrician with a shotgun pointed at his chin with a large amount of heroin in his system.
